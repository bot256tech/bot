# AGRICHAIN 360™ — AYuTe Challenge Pitch Script
## 2 Minutes 30 Seconds | Spoken Presentation

---

### [0:00 - 0:20] THE PROBLEM

Every year, Uganda loses 40 percent of its grain harvest after harvest. That is four billion dollars lost across Sub-Saharan Africa. Not because farmers cannot grow enough — but because they cannot dry, test, certify, or sell their produce properly.

A farmer in Mayuge harvests maize at 25 percent moisture. Without proper drying within 48 hours, aflatoxin develops. The crop is rejected by exporters. The farmer earns 800 shillings per kilo instead of 1,800. That is a 55 percent income gap — caused entirely by post-harvest failure.

---

### [0:20 - 0:50] THE SOLUTION

AGRICHAIN 360 is the digital infrastructure that fixes this.

One platform connecting farmers, buyers, transporters, warehouses, exporters, financial institutions, NGOs, and governments.

Here is how it works in four steps:

**Step one** — A farmer registers via USSD star 284 hash on any phone, or through our mobile app. A village agent maps their farm with GPS. No smartphone needed.

**Step two** — The farmer brings their harvest to our IoT solar dryer. ESP32 sensors monitor temperature, humidity, and moisture every 30 seconds. Real-time data flows to the platform. The farmer watches their drying progress live on their phone.

**Step three** — Once moisture hits 13 percent, the crop is tested for aflatoxin. A Digital Quality Passport is issued — a QR-coded certificate with full traceability from seed to export.

**Step four** — Buyers browse verified produce on our B2B marketplace. They see quality grades, scan QR codes, purchase with escrow protection, and farmers get paid instantly via MTN Mobile Money or Airtel Money.

---

### [0:50 - 1:20] WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT

Three things set us apart:

**First — AI Crop Intelligence.** Our AI advisor answers farming questions in six languages — English, Luganda, Lusoga, Runyankole, Acholi, and Swahili. Farmers upload a photo of a diseased leaf, and our AI identifies the disease, suggests treatment, and estimates recovery probability. Voice-enabled for farmers who prefer speaking to typing.

**Second — IoT Infrastructure.** Our solar dryers are not just dryers. They are data collection points. Every batch generates real-time moisture curves, temperature logs, and automated aflatoxin risk alerts. This data builds a farmer's digital credit profile — enabling them to access loans they could never get before.

**Third — End-to-End Traceability.** Every crop gets a blockchain-style hash chain from seed to consumer. Exporters scan a QR code and see the complete journey — who grew it, where, when it was dried, tested, and certified. This meets EU and Asian export compliance standards.
